Common Questions

How do I delete my account?
Open Soko → tap your profile avatar → scroll down → Delete Account. Your data will be removed within 30 days.

How do I block someone?
Tap their profile → tap the menu icon → Block. They won't be able to see you or contact you.

How do I report someone?
Tap their profile → tap the menu icon → Report. Our team reviews all reports.

Why can't I check in?
Make sure location permissions are enabled and you're within range of the venue. Soko verifies your proximity before allowing check-in.

How do I change my profile?
Tap your profile avatar → Edit Profile. You can update your name, photos, bio, and other details.
