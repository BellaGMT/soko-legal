Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 4, 2026

Introduction

Soko is built to connect people with the world around them. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and what control you have.

1. Data We Collect

Account Information

  • First name, email or phone number
  • Date of birth (age verification)
  • Profile photos (minimum 3 required during account creation)
  • Bio and profile details you choose to add
  • Nod interactions (who you nodded at, mutual matches)

Location Data

  • Approximate location when you open the app — to show nearby venues and activity
  • Precise location temporarily when you check in — used only to verify proximity, then discarded immediately

We do NOT store your precise GPS coordinates. Background location is only used if you opt in via Settings (default OFF).

User Content

  • Messages, thread posts, and event content you create
  • Photos you upload
  • Moments — ephemeral photos and videos you post at venues, visible to other users at that venue for up to 12 hours, then automatically deleted

Usage & Device Data

  • Features you use and screens you visit
  • Device type, OS, and app version (for security and functionality)
  • Push notification token (if you grant notification permission) — used to deliver notifications about matches, messages, and venue activity

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Show you nearby venues, events, and activity
  • Verify you are physically near a venue before check-in
  • Display your profile to people at the same venue (when checked in)
  • Power threads, events, and messaging
  • Security, fraud prevention, and abuse detection
  • Improve the product

We do NOT use your data to serve advertising. We do NOT sell your data.

3. Location — Detailed

When you check in, your device sends your GPS coordinates to our server for a one-time distance check. If you are within range, we record which venue you checked into and when. Your GPS coordinates are immediately discarded — never written to our database.

Other users see only that you are at a named venue. Your exact location is never shared.

Background location access is strictly opt-in. It is only requested if you enable "Background heat updates" in Settings. The default is OFF. If enabled, your anonymous contribution to the live venue density map continues while the app is closed. Others never see your name or coordinates — only aggregate density. You can disable it at any time in Settings.

You can set yourself as "unavailable" in your profile, which hides you from other users' discovery views while checked in. Your check-in remains active for venue statistics but your profile is not shown to other users.

4. How We Share Data

With Other Users

  • Your profile (name, photo, bio) is visible to users at the same venue when you are checked in
  • Your messages are visible to thread participants
  • Events you create are visible to nearby users

With Service Providers

  • Supabase — database, authentication, file storage
  • Google Maps / Google Places — map display, venue search, and proximity verification
  • Google Cloud Vision — automated image moderation
  • Giphy — GIF search
  • Anthropic — automated thread classification
  • Expo — push notification delivery and over-the-air app updates

These providers process data only under our instructions and under signed Data Processing Agreements. We do not sell data to third parties.

5. Data Retention

  • Account and profile: until account deletion
  • Check-in records: 30 days, then permanently deleted
  • Chat messages: chats created through mutual nods expire 7 days after creation. Thread and event messages persist until you delete your account
  • Moments: automatically deleted 12 hours after posting
  • Reports you file: indefinitely (required for moderation)
  • Usage analytics: 12 months

When you delete your account, we delete or anonymize all your personal data within 30 days.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of your data
  • Correction — update your profile at any time
  • Deletion — delete your account via Profile → Delete Account
  • Portability — request your data in a portable format

GDPR (EU users): Our lawful basis for location processing is your consent. For account data, it is performance of our contract with you.

CCPA (California): You have the right to know, delete, and opt out of sale (we do not sell data).

To exercise any rights, email: privacy@getsoko.app

7. Security

  • All data in transit is encrypted with TLS
  • Data at rest is encrypted at the infrastructure level by our database provider
  • Row-level security ensures users can only access their own data
  • Check-in location is validated server-side and never stored
  • Uploaded images are moderated for content safety before storage

8. Children's Privacy

Soko is for users 16 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 16. If we discover a user under 16, we delete their account promptly.

  • Anthropic
  • Expo

10. Changes

We will notify you in-app before significant changes take effect. Continued use constitutes acceptance.
